ABOUT INTRANET

INTRANET

A company intranet

An intranet is a computer network for sharing information, collaboration tools, operational systems, and other computing services within an organization, usually to the exclusion of access by outsiders. The term is used in contrast to public networks, such as the Internet, but uses most of the same technology based on the Internet Protocol Suite. A company-wide intranet can constitute an important focal point of internal communication and collaboration, and provide a single starting point to access internal and external resources. In its simplest form, an intranet is established with the technologies for local area networks (LANs) and wide area networks (WANs). Many modern intranets have search engines, user profiles, blogs, mobile apps with notifications, and events planning within their infrastructure.

Purpose of an intranet

What is Intranet solution

An Introduction

It supports the employees of an organization to communicate, collaborate and perform their tasks in the best possible manner. The core purpose of an intranet solution is to empower employees to work smarter and collaboratively – across the functions, departments, and geographies. In the modern-day digital workplace, companies need an intranet solution that is adaptive to the needs of employees and evolves with the business dynamics. The intranet serves a platform where they can engage, share their ideas, automate their tasks, be up-to-date with the latest company news, and contribute to the company’s goals collectively.
